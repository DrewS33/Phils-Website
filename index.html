Behind the Apron

Meet
Chef Malouf

Some people cook for a living. Chef Malouf cooks because food is the most personal form of hospitality there is.

Based in the Raleigh–Durham area, Chef Malouf has built a private clientele around a simple but demanding standard: every meal should feel considered. Not just nutritious or convenient — though it will be both — but genuinely crafted for the person eating it.

With a background rooted in classical technique and a palate shaped by American, Mediterranean, and Latin influences, Chef Malouf brings restaurant-quality execution into the home kitchen without the formality, pretense, or interruption that comes with dining out.

What clients most often say isn't that the food is impressive — though it is. It's that coming home feels different. The week is handled. One less thing to think about. That's the work Chef Malouf finds most meaningful.

Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What areas do you serve? +
Chef Malouf serves Raleigh, Apex, Cary, Morrisville, Chapel Hill, Durham, and surrounding Triangle communities. Reach out to confirm availability for your specific location.
How does the grocery shopping service work? +
You have two options: provide the ingredients yourself, or add our grocery shopping service ($40 flat fee). If we shop for you, groceries are billed at market cost — there are no markups on ingredients, ever.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or allergies? +
Absolutely. Accommodating dietary needs — from gluten-free and dairy-free to plant-based, low-carb, and specific allergy requirements — is a standard part of our menu planning process, not an exception.
How far in advance do I need to book? +
For weekly meal prep, we recommend at least one week of lead time for your initial consultation and first session. Private dining and event inquiries are best made two to three weeks in advance.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment? +
There is no long-term contract required. Most clients find a consistent weekly rhythm works best, but your schedule is flexible and can be adjusted as needed.
What happens on prep day? +
Chef Malouf arrives at your home, prepares everything fresh in your kitchen, portions and stores all meals, and leaves the kitchen clean. You don't need to be present the entire time — most clients are home for the start and finish.
Do you handle special events and dinner parties? +
Yes. Private dining, intimate dinner parties, and special occasion meals are available by custom quote. Reach out to discuss your vision and we'll build the experience from there.
Can my menu change week to week? +
Menus are rebuilt fresh each week based on your preferences, what's in season, and any changes to your schedule or nutritional goals. Consistency and flexibility coexist here.
